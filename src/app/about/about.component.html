HISTORY


The Agni College of Technology (ACT) was established in the year 2001 by Sri Balaji Charitable and Educational Trust. ACT is one of the top and best college in Chennai for academics and placements. Agni College of Technology is an ISO 9001: 2008 Certified and the courses offered in ACT are recognized by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. UG Level: IT, ECE, Mechanical and CSE are NBA (National Board of Accreditation) accredited. ACT is certified with NAAC with A+ Grade.
